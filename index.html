Fitness Made Simple

Strength/Endurance

As all-in-one total body conditioning tools, strength and endurance training build flexibility and balance alongside strength and endurance. From lean muscle building to extreme weight loss, our EKFA-certified trainers help you achieve your dream body with the help of basic to advanced weight training, kettlebell exercises, and posture correction.

Cardio/Core Conditioning

While cardio gets the heart pumping and benefits the heart, lungs, brain, and muscles, core conditioning improves posture, builds core strength, and gives you that flatter stomach with definition. Our workouts are designed to make cardio fun and core conditioning effective with compound exercises.

HIIT/HHybrid

Hybrid training involves exercises performed in repeated quick bursts at maximum effort, followed by periods of rest or low-activity exercises. Similarly. Our HIIT workouts are carefully crafted to enable healthy and swift weight loss and to avoid any potential injuries related to high-intensity workouts for individuals of all fitness levels.

Mobility/Flexibility

Mobility and flexibility are essential for every individual, be they professional athletes, dancers, or people aiming for fat loss. Having worked with numerous clients from diverse backgrounds, especially athletes, our trainers at Crew are well-versed in sports-specific training, agility, pattern changes, and directional movements.

Obstacle Challenges

Obstacle challenges are adventurous and fun forms of workout that help build strength and improve balance and coordination. It also helps in weight loss. Our obstacle challenge course is designed effectively to train the whole body, improve coordination, and ensure the utmost safety.

Outdoor Activities

Assured to give you Vitamin D, outdoor activities are highly beneficial as they help relieve stress, add variation, and pose a challenge to your workout routine. For our outdoor activities, we keep it dynamic with various activities and exercises all meant to improve your strength, cardiovascular endurance, and flexibility.

Top FAQs

Here is a list of the top FAQs asked by our clients. Maybe this would save you time and clarify your queries in no time.

How do I join the crew in Chennai?

It’s simple Click the “Get Started” button and fill up a few details, and our team of experts will get in touch with you to make your onboarding process easier, smoother, and healthier.

Does the crew in Chennai have branches?

Of course yes. As of now, we have four branches in Chennai and will continuously keep expanding to make your fitness process easier. Available locations: Mogappair, Shenoy Nagar, Kundrathur, and Alwarthirunagar.

Can I join the crew in Chennai if I have heart disease?

Definitely, you can join. Our trainers will provide you with the best fitness activities that will be feasible for your heart condition. Our mantra is “fitness for all." You can book a call with our trainers to get more clarity.

Is functional training suitable for people over 50 years old?

Yes, functional training is suitable for everyone who wishes to stay fit. Age doesn’t matter. Until you can walk on your own, functional training is the best choice for you to stay healthy.

Can I pay the fees on monthly terms?

Yeah, you can choose your feasible mode of payment. But when you choose yearly subscription packages, you will get a higher discount compared to monthly packages. Book a call with our team of experts to choose the right plan.
